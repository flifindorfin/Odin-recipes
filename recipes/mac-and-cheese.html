Binging with Babish Mac and Cheese

This is an easy recipe that can be made with as little as 3 ingredients. It's also insanely cheap and incredibly delicious!

Ingredients

  • Any pasta noodle of your choice!
  • 6 oz of evaporated milk
  • 8 0z of your favorite kind of cheese!

Cooking instructions

  1. Add pasta to a pan, you may use a pot either or works
  2. Fill pan with water until there is just enough to cover the noodles
  3. Bring water to a boil and cook pasta until al dente
  4. Once pasta is cooked add all 6 oz of evaporated milk and stir until some of the milk has cooked off
  5. Finally add the cheese and stir until completely melted
  6. Voila! Easy 3 ingredient Mac and Cheese that takes about 15 minutes to make!
