Easy Chili

This is a recipe that takes about 1 hour to make and is a great food to take to a potluck or party!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of lean ground beef
  • 1 onion diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • chili powder to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 green bell pepper diced
  • 14 oz of crushed tomato
  • 19 oz of red kidney beans
  • 14 oz of diced tomato
  • 1 and a half cups of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a large pot brown beef onion and garlic
  2. Drain fat from beef
  3. Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil
  4. Bring to a simmer and let cook for 45-60 minutes
  5. Serve amd enjoy! Make sure to let any leftovers come to room temp before refrigerating
