Easy Meat Sauce

This is an easy to make meat sauce that you can make in about 20 minutes with a pasta of your choice!

Ingredients

  • Any store bought red sauce
  • Italian Sausage
  • 93 to 7 ground beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Basil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook onions and garlic and let sit until onions are transluscent and garlic is fragrant
  2. Combine ground beef and italian sausage in a large pot
  3. Cook meat until nice brown crust forms
  4. Add Sauce, garlic and basil to the pot
  5. Let simmer for at least 1 hour
  6. Enjoy your easy to make red sauce
